About

I'm a Computer Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. Most of what I build ends up touching both hardware and software. I've written firmware for STM32 and ESP32 boards, designed my own PCBs in KiCad, and put full-stack and AI services into production. The stuff I enjoy most tends to sit right at that boundary, where the code has to actually deal with the physical device it's running on.
